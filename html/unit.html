A Unit is a set of microfilm images.

  • Units can have arbitrary editorial.
  • A Unit's contents can be edited: images removed or added.
  • Certain editors can re-enter the Sorting Room from a Unit.
  • Units can have counter-narrative (TBD).
  • Should we show neighbouring images of the Units?
  • Units can be annotated with a link to any online resource.

The Indigenous Digital Archive is here to help you explore the history of US government Indian boarding schools in the 19th and 20th centuries.

We'd encourage you to join the community to help annotate, research, organise, and even recontextualise these historical documents. Please review our Code of Conduct if you are interested.

This project was initiated by the Museum of Indigenous Arts & Culture in Santa Fe, New Mexico. It is funded by the Knight Foundation.
